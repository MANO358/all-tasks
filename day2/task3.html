Pavan Kalyan

Konidala Pawan Kalyan born Konidala Kalyan Babu 2 September, is an Indian politician and actor who has been serving as the 10th deputy chief minister of Andhra Pradesh since 12 June 2024. He is also serving as the Minister of Panchayat Raj, Rural Development & Rural Water Supply; Environment, Forests, Science & Technology in the Government of Andhra Pradesh. He is the founder and president of the JanaSena Party.As an actor, Kalyan primarily works in Telugu cinema and is known for his unique style and mannerisms. He has a huge fan base and is one of the highest-paid actors of Indian cinema. He has been featured in Forbes India's 100 list multiple times since 2013. Kalyan is the recipient of a Filmfare Award South and a SIIMA Award among other accolades.

Early life and Education

Kalyan was born as Konidala Kalyan Babu on 2 September in a Telugu family to Konidala Venkata Rao and Anjana Devi in bapatala, Andhra Pradesh.[18][19] Venkata Rao worked as a police constable and was transferred on a regular basis. Kalyan was educated at the St. Joseph's High School in Nellore.[20] He was awarded the title "Pawan" during one of his public martial arts presentations.[21] He also holds a black belt in Karate.

Political Carrier

Kalyan started his political career in 2008[53] as the president of Yuvarajyam,[54] the youth wing of Praja Rajyam Party was started by his elder brother Chiranjeevi
. He neither contested in elections nor did he hold any constitutional post during his days in Praja Rajyam Party. While he actively campaigned for the party, he was also hit with few health problems. Kalyan was struck with bouts of vomiting, as he was hit by a sunstroke during a roadshow at Vizag on 19 April 2009.[55] Later, in 2011, when Chiranjeevi merged his party[56] with the Congress Party Kalyan took a break from political life, expressing his silent discontent with his brother's decision to merge the party.
